Character Information
Kratos is the titular protagonist of Santa Monica Studio's God of War series, based on Greek mythology and later, Norse mythology.
Atreus is the son of Kratos, a Greek divine being. Atreus has a tremendous physiology, making him a hybrid of the Greek and Norse pantheon.
Freya is a supporting character in God of War. She is a Vanir goddess who helps Kratos and Atreus on their journey.
Baldur was the Norse Aesir God of Light and the main antagonist of God of War.He was the son of Odin and Freya.
Mimir also known as the Smartest Man Alive, is the Norse God of Knowledge and Wisdom and an ally of Kratos and Atreus.
